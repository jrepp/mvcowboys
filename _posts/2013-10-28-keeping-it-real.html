What better way to bring in the Christmas Holiday Season than bringing home a real Christmas Tree!

This season, come support the Mission Viejo Cowboys' Annual Christmas Tree Fundraiser!! The event, arranged by Trees for A Cause, is simple and fun, plus you get to hand pick the Christmas Tree of your choice. Don't delay, orders must be received by today for a fresh and on-time delivery!

It's easy as 1, 2, 3:

1. Pre-Order the type of Christmas tree you'd like delivered.
2. Meet us at La Paz Middle School on December 7th (8am - 12 pm) to select and pickup your tree, or arrange for local delivery to address of your choice. Click here for pickup location.
3. Decorate and Enjoy your New Christmas Tree!

Click here to easily complete your Order Online! 

Printable order forms are also available here; simply give the form to a Team Representative.

Feel free to visit the Trees for a Cause website for more information about their trees and how they are helping families bring in the Holiday Season.

Not in need of a tree for your own home? Perhaps you know of a family who would be grateful for a Christmas Tree Donation for their home!

Happy Holidays from the MV Cowboys!!
