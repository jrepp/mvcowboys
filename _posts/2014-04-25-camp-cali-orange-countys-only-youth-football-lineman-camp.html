For Ages 8 & Up

Location: La Paz Middle School

Dates: May 20th – July 10th

Time: 5:30p- 7:30P

Cost: $260

Session: Weekly -Tues, Wed, Thurs.

 

The Camp is designed to help prepare first time & veteran’s football players for the upcoming 2014-football season.

 

Camp Cali will help improve and develop lineman skills in the following areas:

  • Basic footwork
  • Blocking assignments
  • Blocking Techniques
  • Conditioning

 

Coaching:

All coaches who are participating in Camp Cali have been background checked and have multiple years of youth football coaching experience. Our staff has won multiple league championships as well as OCJAFF & PCC Super bowls at different levels.

 

Camp Cali invites football players from PCC, POP Warner & OCJAFF to participate. Everyone is welcome to take advantage of the camp.

 

Don’t miss out on this opportunity. Space is limited. First come, first served.
